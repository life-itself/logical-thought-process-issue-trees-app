How it works

Turn noticing into a map.

  1. 01
    Name the goal

    What is this person, group, or commons for?

  2. 02
    Offer a contribution

    An action, insight, or critique becomes visible.

  3. 03
    Show where it fits

    Placed in relation to a living goal map.

  4. 04
    Give the reason

    Why does it fit? What does it support, challenge, or reveal?

  5. 05
    Invite checks

    Others say what holds, what breaks, what needs revision.

  6. 06
    Update the map

    When reality answers, the map changes.

  7. 07
    Return what matters

    When an old conversation becomes relevant again, prior learning comes back at the right moment.
